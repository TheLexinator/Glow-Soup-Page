Golden Glow Soup

"You are what you eat, eat well."


INGREDIENTS

  • Olive Oil
  • 8 Carrots (chopped)
  • 2 Leeks (chopped)
  • 1 Red Pepper (chopped)
  • 1 tsp Turmeric
  • 1 Tbsp Coriander
  • Half tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • Vegetable Stock
  • 1 Can Coconut Cream

DIRECTIONS

  1. SautÃ© chopped vegetables in some olive oil for 15 minutes
  2. Add spices and stir for 2 minutes
  3. Add 5 cups of vegetable broth (stock cubes can also be used) and bring to a boil
  4. Blend with an immersion blender
  5. Add Coconut cream
  6. Enjoy with some toast.
